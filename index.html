🏰 Game Overview

Kingshot is a medieval city-building and strategy survival game by Century Games. As a Governor, you rebuild a fallen kingdom, develop your city, battle Cesares rebels, and coordinate with your alliance to dominate kingdom-wide events.

Three Pillars of Success

1. Hero Investment — Shards are scarce. Wrong investments can't be fixed cheaply. Set alliance-wide policies.

2. Rally Discipline — Captain selection and joiner presets directly determine event damage output.

3. Calendar Warfare — Top alliances plan the week before events start, not during battle hour.

Key Progression Milestones

TC 7 → Alliance unlocked

TC 10 → Alliance Mobilization eligible

TC 19 + CC 1 → Mystic Trial unlocked

TC 20 → Mastery Forging unlocked

TC 25+ → Governor Charms system

TC 30+ → Truegold upgrades

💡 The Golden Rule: Shards and Forgehammers are permanently scarce. Never invest in heroes or gear without knowing it aligns with your alliance's rally role policy.

⚔ Hero System

Hero Roles — The Most Important Concept

🗡 Rally Captain

All 9 of the captain's hero skills apply in a rally. Rally damage is primarily determined by captain heroes and skill trigger chance.

Invest heavily: best gear, Forgehammers, skill manuals. Limit captains to a small, heavily-supported roster.

🛡 Rally Joiner

Only the first hero's first expedition skill is eligible for joiner skill selection. Only ~4 joiner skills apply per rally.

Higher-level skills displace weaker ones — wrong joiner heroes can overwrite better buffs. Standardize one preset for all members.

🏰 Garrison Defender

The player with the highest stat bonuses provides the defense bonus source. 4 primary skills from other garrison members apply.

Best offense heroes ≠ best garrison heroes. Widget orientation matters.

💰 Economy Heroes

Blue/rare heroes with gathering speed and resource expedition skills. Separate investment track — do NOT spend combat materials on them.

Upgrade for economy, not combat power rating.

🐻 Bear Hunt

How It Works

R4+ alliance members start the Bear Hunt. Alliance sets a trap; members join rallies to deal damage. Rewards depend on personal damage contribution and captain quality.

⚠️ Leaving your alliance clears your Bear Hunt ranking points. There is also a minimum interval between sessions for participation across alliances.

Alliance Leader Checklist

✅ Choose a time window when top captains are active (captain heroes drive output)
✅ Pre-designate and publish captain rotation
✅ Publish ONE standardized joiner preset + "send troops only" fallback
✅ Teach members to view rally bonus contributors — remove incorrect contributors via rally UI
✅ Use trap scheduling / auto-trap to reduce missed sessions across time zones

Step-by-Step Rally Execution

Step 1: Captains start continuous rallies. Multiple rallies can run simultaneously — constraint is member march cycles, not rally cap.

Step 2: Joiners join in waves with minimum travel time. Long march times reduce total hits per event window — maximize rally cycles.

Step 3: Keep joiner skill slots clean. Limited joiner skills apply; higher-level or incorrect skills can displace better ones.

⚔ Kingdom vs Kingdom

Kingdom of Power

Cross-kingdom battle event. Points only earned when level difference between you and opponent is within 3 levels. For rallies, level difference is based on the rally initiator vs opponent.

PhaseTiming
MatchmakingSaturday (results Sunday)
Preparation~5 days + 10 hours
BattleSaturday 10:00 UTC
💡 Lock a scoring plan around the official "within 3 levels" rule so members don't waste attacks on ineligible targets. Stock teleports and shields before battle window.

Castle Battle

First alliance to occupy King's Castle for 2.5 continuous hours wins. If no winner after 5 hours, longest total occupation wins. An Alert Zone rebellion triggers before the protection shield expires.

ℹ️ Castle Battle is an endurance and logistics contest. Enlistment Office and infirmary overflow management are decisive for longer hold strategies.

Entry Readiness — Clear These Before Battle

You will be blocked from entering if: troops are in the infirmary · troops are gathering · troops are garrisoned · you have active attacks · Guard Station is burning.

Buffs That Apply in Swordland

✅ Skin bonuses · ✅ Position Perks & King's Skills · ✅ Combat-related Outpost buffs

Matchmaking uses top-20 squad power among combatants + subs, derived from Governor Gear, Hero Gear, and Battle Tech. Reducing combatant count does NOT affect matchmaking.

Healing During Battle

Leave the battlefield to heal. Return takes 12 minutes in a 1-hour match — time this carefully. Healing speed-ups are provided free during battle.

🏛 Sanctuary Battles

Eligibility & Rules

R4+ signs up. Alliance needs 20+ members. One Sanctuary + one Fortress per phase. Auto sign-up next phase if you currently hold it.

⚠️ Alliance territory battle buffs do NOT apply at Sanctuary/Fortress locations. Do not expect territory buffs during Sanctuary defense.

The Win Condition

Continuously occupy for 30 minutes = full control. If nobody completes 30 minutes, the last alliance occupying at battle end wins. Many alliances lose by trading control too frequently and never completing the continuous hold.

Participation Rewards

Rewards may be gated to the first governors who defeat NPC rebels in the sanctuary. Prevent alliance members from solo "tagging" NPCs that steal the first-clear trigger. If over-stacking a rally, members who deal no damage may receive no points.

Defense Phase

After capture, a shield activates — no attacks. NPCs will reset for the next battle and must be defeated again to take control. Brief your members on this after every capture.

Leader Checklist

✅ Confirm R4+ signed up + alliance has 20+ members
✅ Set 3 time-slot options (final time determined by ALL registered alliances' votes, not just yours)
✅ Communicate first-NPC-clear protocol
✅ Plan rotation to hold 30 continuous minutes without resetting the timer
✅ Account for auto-sign-up next phase if currently holding

ℹ️ Assign R4 to multiple reliable, time-zone-distributed officers — never concentrate event start authority in one person's availability.

Core Alliance Policies

Rally ruleset: Publish and enforce standardized joiner presets. Rally etiquette is not soft culture — it directly determines damage output and rewards.

Buff validity tables: Per event, publish which buffs apply and which don't (Mystic Trial and Tri-Alliance Clash have major invalidation rules).

Membership stability: Alliance rewards require being a current member at tally. Bear Hunt ranking points are cleared when leaving. Build a stable, active roster.

Captain support policy: Limit rally captain designations; aggressively support them with Forgehammers, Mithril, skill manuals, and gear priority.

💰 F2P Strategy

Top F2P Mistakes to Avoid

⚠️ Don't invest in the wrong hero. Shards can't be easily recovered. Know your role (joiner vs captain) before spending any shard resource.
⚠️ Don't scatter Forgehammers. Focus on your designated rally captain's gear set first. Every Forgehammer on a non-captain is a wasted multiplier.
⚠️ Don't buy gems for shields. 24h gem shields are premium — time your city activity to avoid needing them.

Daily F2P Habits

✅ Push Rebel Suppression progress (increases passive gold income permanently)
✅ Join Bear Hunt rallies with correct standardized joiner preset
✅ Complete Alliance Mobilization missions (9 default — don't abandon, costs an attempt)
✅ Stock healing speed-ups before any war event
✅ Participate in Mystic Trial weekly (Raid rewards compound each week after first unlock)

F2P Gem Priority

1. VIP level (passive multiplier on everything)
2. Alliance help requests during construction
3. Speed-ups for time-sensitive event windows
4. Avoid gem shields — play defensively with teleports instead
5. Never buy random hero summons — target specific hero shards
