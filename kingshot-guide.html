Game Overview

Kingshot is a medieval idle strategy-survival game by Century Games. You're a Governor in a collapsed dynasty, tasked with rebuilding civilization — managing resources, training armies, recruiting heroes, and competing for dominance against other governors.

Core Gameplay Loop
  • Upgrade your Town Center to unlock buildings & features
  • Build & upgrade resource buildings for passive income
  • Recruit and level up heroes to lead armies
  • Train troops and participate in events for rewards
  • Join an alliance to access cooperative events
  • Compete in KvK (Kingdom vs Kingdom) for glory
Key Currencies
  • Gems — Premium currency; save for Hero Roulette
  • Gold / Food / Wood / Stone — Basic building materials
  • Speedups — Time accelerators; save for events
  • Forge Hammers — For Mythic gear upgrades (Bear Hunt source)
  • Truegold — Late-game upgrade material (unlocks ~day 70+)
  • Hero Shards / Keys — For summoning & ascending heroes
🔑 The Golden Rule Don't spread resources thin. Focus on 3 core heroes, save gems for Hero Roulette, and save speedups for event windows. These three habits separate top players from everyone else.

City Building Guide

Building Priority Order

Always upgrade Town Center (TC) first — it gates everything else. You cannot upgrade any building above your current TC level.

🥇 Top Priority Buildings
  • Town Center — Always upgrade first; unlocks all other buildings
  • Academy — Start research immediately; construction speed first
  • Storehouse — Protects resources from raids; upgrade before resource wall
  • Hospital / Infirmary — Recovers wounded troops instead of losing them
  • Barracks / Training Camps — Needed for troop training & upgrades
🥈 Secondary Buildings
  • Farms, Lumber Mills, Quarries, Mines — Passive resource income
  • Command Center — Unlocks march capacity upgrades
  • Embassy — Alliance reinforcements & help features
  • War Academy — Combat research bonuses
  • Watchtower — Intel Missions (hero XP, gear scraps)

Builder Management

  • You start with 1 builder. Never leave it idle.
  • The 2nd builder queue (purchased with gems) is the only non-Hero-Roulette gem purchase worth making.
  • Do short upgrades while active, long upgrades (8–12h+) while sleeping or away.
  • Use Free Speedups from events and missions to clear upgrade queues.

Governor Gear & Charms

Governor Gear (Unlocks at TC 22)

Provides Attack & Defense bonuses to your Governor. Can be upgraded up to level 26. Found in your Profile section.

Governor Charms (Unlocks at TC 25)

Increases Lethality & Health — the two most important combat stats. Max level 22. Craft from the Profile section using Charm Items earned in events like Eternity's Reach.

Hero System

Heroes are the most important progression system in Kingshot. They lead your troops, define your combat power, and unlock game modes. Focus on 3 core heroes only.

Hero Development Methods

⬆ Leveling (XP)

Use Hero XP Potions from Intel Missions and events. Increases base stats (ATK/DEF/HP) and troop march capacity. Max level is 80 (requires TC 26).

⭐ Ascending (Stars)

Use Hero Shards & Keys from Hero Roulette. Ascending unlocks new skills, improves existing ones, and boosts base stats significantly. Always prioritize ascending your 3 main heroes.

🔧 Skill Upgrades

Use Hero Skill Tomes to upgrade individual skills. Improves damage multipliers, cooldowns, and buff durations. Required to unlock higher skill tiers alongside hero level.

Hero Roles

🗡 Rally Leader

Leads alliances in Bear Hunt, Castle Battle, KvK. Their gear and skills define rally power. Needs high hero level, maxed gear, and strong widget investment.

🤝 Rally Joiner

Joins someone else's rally and contributes troops. Hero gear does NOT apply when joining — only troop count (based on hero level) matters. Focus on getting joiners to 4 stars for their passive skills.

🛡 Garrison

Defends a building or castle. Different stat weightings apply. Key garrison heroes have specific skills that activate while defending.

Skill Types

🗺 Expedition Skills

Active during Rallies and open-world combat. Provide army-wide buffs. Scale with Widgets. These are what matter in major events.

⚔ Conquest Skills

Governs performance in PvP Arena and Conquest game modes. Focus on burst damage and crowd control. Less relevant for large-scale war events.

Widgets (Multiplicative Bonuses)

ℹ Widget System Widgets provide multiplicative bonuses up to +15% at max level — making them one of the most efficient power upgrades in the game. There are two types: Rally Squad Widgets (only active when hero leads a rally) and Defender Squad Widgets (only active when garrisoning). Use the right widget type for your hero's role.

Hero Gear Guide

Hero Gear is the single biggest power multiplier in Kingshot. Fully upgraded gear can add millions of troop power. But gear only applies when you lead a rally — not when you join one.

Gear Rarity Tiers & Max Levels

RarityMax LevelNotes
⬜ Grey (Common)20Merge into higher tiers
🟩 Green (Uncommon)40—
🟦 Blue (Rare)60—
🟪 Purple (Epic)80⚠ Don't consume Epic for XP — it's hard to get
🟨 Mythic (Gold)100 + Mastery ForgingMastery Forging unlocks at Lv 20; uses Forgehammers for 1.1x–2x+ multipliers
🟥 Red (Endgame)200Requires Mythic Lv 100 + Mastery 10 + 2 extra Mythics. Unlocks ~day 40

Gear Stat Priority

Priority Order (Most to Least Important)
  1. Infantry Health (Chest & Gloves) — Keeps frontline alive longer
  2. Archer Lethality — Highest damage dealer; lethality = final damage multiplier
  3. Cavalry Lethality
  4. Infantry Lethality
  5. Archer Health
  6. Cavalry Health

Upgrade Strategy

  • Pick your main march (top 3 heroes) before upgrading any gear
  • Strip gear from heroes outside your main march and reforge it for resources
  • Don't level gear equally — focus priority pieces first
  • Infantry Tank: boost Health. Cavalry & Archers: boost Lethality
  • Push main march gear to Lv 80–100; secondary heroes to Lv 40–60
  • Reforge (not Consume) leveled gear you don't need to recover full resources
💡 Joiner Hero Gear Hero gear doesn't apply when joining a rally. What matters for joiners is: (1) troop count from hero XP level, and (2) passive skills like Chenko's Stand of Arms aura. Get joiner heroes to 4 stars to unlock their key passive — don't waste gear resources on them.

Troop Guide

Troop Types

🛡 Infantry

Frontline tanks. Takes damage to protect your Archers and Cavalry. Highest health pool, lowest DPS. Essential for survivability.

🏹 Archers

Highest DPS in the game. Ideal for Bear Hunt and open-world combat. Should make up majority of your march formation in late game.

🐴 Cavalry

Mobile flankers with strong counter-class bonuses vs Archers. Mid-tier damage. Useful in specific counter scenarios.

Optimal Troop Formations for Bear Hunt

StageInfantryCavalryArchers
Gen 1 Heroes10%30%60%
Gen 2 Heroes10%20%70%
Gen 3 Heroes10%10%80%
Gen 4–5 (Universal)1%10%89%

The 1/10/89 formation is the gold standard for maximum Bear Hunt damage once you have Gen 4+ heroes.

Training Tips

  • Always keep training camps active — never let them sit idle
  • Use Troop Training Events with 2x speed items for maximum efficiency
  • Train troops non-stop; a reserve of troops is your biggest KvK asset
  • Higher TC level unlocks higher-tier troops (T11 Archers are the late-game standard)
  • Keep your Hospital / Infirmary upgraded — wounded troops are recoverable, dead troops are not

Island Guide

The Island is a separate development zone that provides passive combat bonuses to your troops and heroes. It's often overlooked by newer players but provides significant stat multipliers.

Island Building Priority

Priority Order
  • Infantry Defense buildings first — increases health/survivability of frontline
  • Archer Attack buildings second — Archers are your main DPS source
  • Cavalry buildings third
  • Match Island upgrades to your primary troop composition
ℹ Island Synergy Island stat priorities should mirror your Gear, Research, War Academy, and Charm priorities. The same stat priority applies everywhere: Infantry Health → Archer Lethality → Cavalry Lethality → Infantry Lethality → Archer Health → Cavalry Health.

Island Resources & Activities

  • Complete Island missions and quests for bonus materials
  • Participate in Island-specific events when they occur
  • Coordinate with alliance for Island territory control events
  • Use Island buildings to supplement power gains when Town Center upgrades slow down

Growth Missions (5-Day Events)

A 5-day event where daily missions unlock progressively. Complete them for significant rewards — most tasks align with regular gameplay (like "Upgrade TC to Level X"). Never miss Growth Mission events — the rewards are the best return-per-effort in the game.

Eternity's Reach

Stage-based solo event that awards Charm Items — the primary resource for Charm upgrades. With the right hero composition, F2P players can reach the leaderboard. Prioritize this event to power up your Charms, which provide massive late-game combat stat multipliers.

Bear Hunt (Pitfall Event)

⚠ Critical Warning Never miss Bear Hunt. Forge Hammers (required for Mythic gear upgrades) have no substitute source. Missing Bear Hunts permanently lags your gear progression behind other players.

How It Works

  • Scheduled by R4/R5 alliance leaders — runs every 2 days with 2 trap slots
  • Before the event: complete Intel Missions to gather Arrowheads and donate to the Bear Trap to increase alliance-wide attack multiplier
  • Event runs for exactly 30 minutes; final rally can be called 5 min before end
  • Rewards are based on personal damage dealt and alliance total contribution
  • Building Placement: The Bear Trap can be placed anywhere within Alliance territory

Rally Leader Setup

  • Use maximum Archer-heavy formation (1/10/89 for Gen 4+)
  • Gear your rally leader fully (gear only matters when leading, not joining)
  • Prioritize Archer Lethality and Archer Attack from all sources: Gear, Island, Research, Charms, Governor Gear
  • Check heroes and troops of joiners before starting — remove anyone with wrong bonuses
  • Use secondary marches with heroes like Chenko to boost other rallies' Lethality

Alliance Management (R4/R5 Tips)

Poll members for best time slots and trigger both Trap 1 and Trap 2 at different times to maximize participation. Group rally leaders tightly around the Bear Trap for visual organization.

Solving Instant-Fill Problem (Formula):
Take Rally Capacity (e.g., 1,132,210) → Subtract Rally Leader's Squad Capacity (e.g., 162,210) → Divide remaining by number of expected joiners → Set this as the joiner troop limit. This distributes damage fairly across all members.

Joiner Tips

  • Hero gear doesn't apply as a joiner — focus on troop count via hero XP level
  • Get joiner heroes (especially Chenko) to 4 stars to unlock their key passive skill
  • Chenko's "Stand of Arms" (+Lethality aura) is one of the best joiner skills in the game
  • Respect alliance joiner troop limits — breaking them hurts collective damage

Kingdom vs Kingdom (KvK)

KvK (also called Kingdom of Power) is the largest event in Kingshot — two kingdoms face off over multiple phases. Your kingdom becomes eligible when it reaches approximately day 70. It recurs every ~4 weeks.

Matchmaking

The system matches your kingdom with one of similar power, calculated from the top 100 players and server generation. Removing or unequipping gear does not affect matchmaking power. If no suitable match is found, your kingdom receives Matchmaking Bye Rewards instead.

KvK Phases

Phase 1 — 5 Days
Preparation Phase
Points via upgrades & training Daily Phase Ranking Rewards Up to 5 State Medals

Earn points by upgrading buildings, training troops, and completing tasks. Unlock 3 Personal Reward Chests per day (final chest ≈ 200K points). Top 2000 players get daily Phase Ranking Rewards. Top 50 alliances get Alliance Phase Ranking Rewards.

Phase 2 — 12 Hours
Battle Phase
10:00–22:00 UTC Cross-Kingdom Teleporters unlock at 10:00 6th State Medal from Castle Battle

The losing Preparation Phase kingdom defends; the winner attacks. Cross-Kingdom Teleporters unlock at 10:00 UTC. You only earn points attacking players within 3 TC levels of you. Castle positions and King titles reset at 12:00 UTC for the Castle Battle.

KvK Rewards Shop

Spend Kingdom Coins in the KvK Shop for exclusive items: Sky Guardian Avatar Frame, Truegold, Mythic General Hero Shards, and other high-value items. Prioritize Truegold and Hero Shards for maximum progression value.

KvK Preparation Checklist

  • Hoard speedups — use them during Preparation Phase for construction/training points
  • Train troops leading up to KvK (Hospital must be full-capacity first)
  • Upgrade Storehouse before KvK — lose nothing above capacity to enemy raids
  • Coordinate with alliance on attack/defense assignments
  • Identify high-value rally targets and avoid punching above your power tier
  • Scout enemy cities before attacking — check if troops are away and gates are open

Alliance Guide

🔑 Join an Alliance Immediately Joining an active alliance at day 1 gives access to: help-speed building boosts, Bear Hunt (your Forge Hammer source), Alliance Championship weekly events, Castle Battle rewards, and reinforcement support during attacks.

Alliance Ranks (R1–R5)

R4OfficerSchedules Bear Hunt traps, manages events, rally coordination
R3Elite MemberTrusted veterans, help with rally coordination
R2MemberFull event participation rights
R1RecruitProbationary period

Alliance Championship (Weekly Event)

A weekly competitive event between alliances. Follow your alliance's strategy for optimal results — coordination is key. The 2-1 win strategy (win the first two rounds, hold positions) is considered the gold standard for consistent placement rewards.

Tri-Alliance Clash

Three alliances fight over map buildings for 1 hour (Saturday). Phases: Preparation (3 min) → Seize & Conquer (17 min) → Garrison Occupation (20 min). The alliance with the most points wins. Use the Tri-Alliance Clash Map (available at kingshotguides.com) to plan your battle strategy.

King's Castle Battle

Occurs every 2 weeks. An alliance must hold the Castle for 3 hours straight or have the longest total hold time within 6 hours. Winning grants your alliance the right to appoint the King, unlocking kingdom-wide buffs and exclusive cosmetics. This is one of the most important recurring alliance events.

Progression Roadmap

Phase 1 — Days 1–14
Foundation (TC 1–10)
Target: 500K–1M Power Goal: Rush to TC 10
  • TC 7 unlocks second builder — buy it with gems (the #1 gem priority)
  • TC 8 unlocks higher-tier troops | TC 10 unlocks alliance features
  • Research Construction Speed first in Academy
  • Join an alliance immediately for help speed & Bear Hunt access
  • Do NOT invest heavily in Gen 1 heroes beyond what's needed
  • Do NOT spend gems on anything except the 2nd builder right now
Phase 2 — Days 14–40
Growth (TC 11–19)
Target: 2M–5M Power Goal: Economic infrastructure
  • TC 15: Resource demands start increasing — keep building upgrades queued
  • TC 16–18: Start a farm account to supplement resource income
  • TC 19: Mystic Trial unlocks — critical source of Mithril and Misty Crystals
  • Save gems for your first Hero Roulette (need 162K)
  • Identify your 3 core heroes and stop investing in others
  • Never miss Bear Hunt from this point forward
Phase 3 — Days 40–110
Acceleration (TC 20–25)
Target: 8M–20M Power Goal: Hero maxing + KvK prep
  • TC 20: Event participation becomes critical
  • TC 22–23: At least one maxed hero by now
  • TC 24 Wall: Costs roughly double — start hoarding speedups at TC 20
  • TC 25: Governor Charms unlock — prioritize Lethality & Health charms
  • Invest in Hero Gear via Bear Hunt Forge Hammers
  • Start combat research for KvK preparation
  • Resource protection matters now — you're a raid target
Phase 4 — Days 110+
Endgame (TC 26–30+)
Target: 25M+ Power Goal: Truegold + KvK dominance
  • TC 26–27: Truegold becomes the primary bottleneck resource
  • TC 26: Heroes can reach max level 80
  • TC 28–29: Tempered Truegold gates further upgrades
  • Focus Truegold on the unlock path — don't spread it across multiple buildings
  • Governor Charms are the biggest single-source stat boost — don't neglect them
  • T11 Archers + maxed gear + Red gear are the endgame standards

F2P & Low-Spender Strategy

⚠ Top 5 Progression Mistakes (Avoid These!)
  1. Investing in too many heroes — 3 strong heroes > 8 weak ones. Period.
  2. Spending gems outside Hero Roulette — Only exception is the 2nd builder. Save everything else for Hero Roulette (need 162K for a full spin cycle).
  3. Using speedups outside events — Speedups during construction events earn 3x+ rewards. Hoard every speedup until the right window.
  4. Neglecting Bear Hunt — Your Forge Hammer supply depends entirely on this event.
  5. Ignoring the Storehouse — Resources above capacity are vulnerable every moment. Upgrade it before the TC 20+ resource rush.

Free Resource Sources

Daily Habits (30 min/day)
  • Daily missions — consistent gold, resources, speedups + bonus chest
  • Intel missions (Watchtower) — hero XP potions + gear scraps
  • Growth Missions — huge milestone rewards
  • Alliance help requests — reduces build time via alliance members
  • Login rewards — claim every day
Long-Term F2P Income
  • Bear Hunt — Forge Hammers (non-negotiable, always participate)
  • Eternity's Reach — Charm Items for F2P charm grind
  • Hero Roulette events — save scrolls & gems for the event window
  • Suppress Mode — rare resources (Mithril, Misty Crystals)
  • Farm Account — second account to donate resources to your main

Suppress Mode

Turn-Based Game Mode

A turn-based combat mode using 6 heroes. Each hero has Suppress-specific skills in addition to their Expedition skills. As heroes take turns, their ultimate skill meter fills — click the glowing icon to unleash ultimate attacks. Features 2x speed and auto-battle for cleared stages. Rewards include Mithril and Misty Crystals that are hard to find elsewhere. Push as far as you can.
